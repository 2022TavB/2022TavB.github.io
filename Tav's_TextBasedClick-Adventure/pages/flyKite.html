The Strange Elf Adventure

You decide to help your brother.

You take the kite from your brother and start to run back with it. Just as you are about to turn to watch your step you trip over a root. As you fall you let go of the kite, watching it soar in the air. Your brother doesn't seem to notice. Your trip knocks you into a berry bush, you bonk your head on the branches and you're out.

Watch where you step next time.
